# War Crimes

This layer displays documented war crimes committed during the People's Liberation War in Yugoslavia (1941-1945).

## About This Data

The markers on the map represent locations where war crimes were documented. Each marker contains:
- **Location**: The place where the crime occurred
- **Date**: When the crime was committed
- **Description**: Details about the incident
- **Sources**: References and documentation

## Important Notes

- This data is based on historical records and may be incomplete
- Some locations are approximate due to limited historical documentation
- New research may reveal additional information or correct existing entries

## Filtering

War crimes are filtered based on the timeline slider, showing only those crimes that occurred within the selected date range.
