## Purpose

The NOB (Narodnooslobodilaƒçki rat - People's Liberation War) Map Project is an interactive historical visualization tool designed to document and display the military formations, movements, battles, and events during the Yugoslav People's Liberation War (1941-1945).

## Features

### Interactive Map Layers
- **Military Formations**: View the locations and formation dates of Detachments, Brigades, Divisions, and Corps
- **Campaign Movements**: Trace the movement paths of brigades throughout the war
- **Battles**: Explore major battles and military engagements
- **War Crimes**: Document locations of war crimes during the conflict
- **Occupied Territory**: Visualize the territorial control during the occupation

### Timeline Filtering
The timeline slider allows you to filter all map layers by date range, showing only the formations, battles, and events that existed or occurred during your selected time period.

### Search Functionality
Quickly find specific military units by name using the search tool.

## How to Use

1. **Select a Category**: Click on one of the main menu buttons (Calendar, Formations, Campaigns, About)
2. **Choose a Layer**: For Formations and Campaigns, select a specific layer from the submenu
3. **Filter by Time**: Use the Calendar button to activate the timeline slider and filter by date range
4. **Explore Details**: Click on map markers to view detailed information in the sidebar
5. **View Campaign Trails**: When viewing brigades with campaign data, click on a marker and then use the campaign button to view their movement trail

## Data Sources and Limitations

### Sources
This project is based on:
- Historical military records
- Academic research and publications
- Official archives
- Wikipedia and other documented sources

### Known Limitations
- **Coordinate Approximations**: Some locations may be approximate due to incomplete historical records or changes in geographical boundaries
- **Incomplete Data**: Not all units, battles, or events may be documented
- **Research Constraints**: Limited access to certain archives or documentation may result in gaps
- **Translation Issues**: Original sources may have been in various languages, potentially leading to naming inconsistencies

## Development

This website was created using:
- **Frontend**: Leaflet.js for mapping, vanilla JavaScript
- **Backend**: Node.js with Express
- **Database**: MySQL with spatial data support
- **Development Assistance**: GitHub Copilot
