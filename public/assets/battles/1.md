# Battle of Split 1943

## Overview
The Battle of Split 1943 was a significant military engagement during World War II in the Dalmatian region of Yugoslavia.

## Details
- **Start Date**: September 11, 1943
- **End Date**: October 2, 1943
- **Location**: Outskirts of Split, near the village of Klis

## Historical Context
Following the Italian armistice in September 1943, Yugoslav Partisans moved to secure Italian-held territories in Dalmatia. The battle for Split was part of broader operations to liberate the Adriatic coast from Axis occupation.

## Significance
This battle was crucial for establishing Partisan control over the major Dalmatian city of Split and its surrounding areas.
